Calories Calculator

To lose weight, you need to eat fewer calories than you expend over time. Thus, learning how many calories you
“burn” every day is one of the most important steps you can take to lose weight. Use this calculator to estimate
how many calories you burn and how many you should eat every day to lose weight.
